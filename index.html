About Us

The kudawala plays a vital role in the community by collecting waste from households and businesses. After gathering the materials, they sort and sell them to local vendors, promoting recycling and supporting the local economy. This unique system not only helps reduce waste but also fosters a sense of responsibility among residents about their environmental impact. Through their efforts, kudawala contribute to a cleaner, more sustainable neighborhood while creating economic opportunities.
